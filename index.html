About Surplus2Serve

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a sustainable platform that bridges the gap between businesses with surplus food and organizations that can distribute it to those in need, reducing food waste and combating hunger.

Our Vision

A world where no surplus food goes to waste, and every person has access to nutritious meals. We envision a community working together for social good.

How It Works

Simple Steps for Contributors & Recipients

For Donors & Businesses

1
Create Account

Register as a donor with your business details and contact information.

2
List Surplus Food

Post available surplus food with quantity, quality, and pickup/delivery details.

3
Get Matched

NGOs and organizations view your listing and request the food donation.

4
Make Impact

Arrange delivery/pickup and track your donation impact in real-time.

For NGOs & Recipients

1
Register Organization

Sign up with your NGO details, mission, and beneficiary information.

2
Browse Donations

View available surplus food listings from donors and businesses near you.

3
Request & Confirm

Request donations and coordinate pickup/delivery with donors.

4
Distribute & Report

Distribute food to beneficiaries and provide feedback to donors.
